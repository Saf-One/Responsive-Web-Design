The Life of Albert Einstein

  • 1879 - Born in Ulm, Germany
  • 1905 - Publishes his theory of special relativity
  • 1915 - Completes his theory of general relativity
  • 1921 - Awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics
  • 1933 - Emigrates to the United States
  • 1939 - Writes to President Roosevelt about the possibility of an atomic bomb
  • 1955 - Dies in Princeton, New Jersey
